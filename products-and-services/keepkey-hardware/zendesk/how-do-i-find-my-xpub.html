How Do I Find My xPub?

To export your xPub on KeepKey, you can try this 3rd party tool, called the KK Xpub Exporter: https://kkxpubexporter-mv2ds3ul8.now.sh/.

Note, the tool is not a ShapeShift product, but we have recommended it to users in the past. Users have seen success exporting their Xpub using this tool. If you have issues with the tool, please leave a comment on the Github page here: https://github.com/keepkey/keepkey.js/pull/24#issuecomment-460348006
