Can I Use KeepKey with My Mobile Device?

You can use your KeepKey with the MyCelium app. Download MyCelium from the Google Play store or the App Store. Be aware that KeepKey connects to your phone via an OTG adapter cable. If your phone is unable to connect to an OTG adapter cable, you will not be able to use your KeepKey with your mobile device.
