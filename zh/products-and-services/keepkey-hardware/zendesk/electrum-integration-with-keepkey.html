Electrum Integration with KeepKey

Please follow these instructions to integrate Electrum with your KeepKey device.

First download Electrum via this link: https://electrum.org/#download

First, select "Standard wallet":

 

Select "Use a hardware device":
 

Connect your KeepKey device - hit "next"
 

Select your device:
 

Enter your current pin:
 

Select legacy (p2pkh): 

If you'd like to select a different account on your KeepKey device please follow the the derivation path guidline below:

Account #0: m/44'/0'/0'

Account #1: m/44'/0'/1'

Account #2: m/44'/0'/2'

Account #3: m/44'/0'/3'
